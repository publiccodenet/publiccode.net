Codebases we work with

CircuLaw

Stage 1: Assessment

What it is

CircuLaw is a knowledge platform dedicated to enabling the transition to a circular economy by identifying opportunities in current law to support a circular future.

close

How we're helping

We're helping launch the CircuLaw open source ecosystem by:

  • Providing feedback and guidance on process
  • Supporting sustainable development

Replicating:
For now, the codebase is deployed with a configuration for the Dutch jurisdiction. The community aims to expand the horizons by exploring the applicability of CircuLaw in a different legal contexts, and are taking a closer look at the influence of European Union law. In doing so, the CircuLaw project will tap into an entire new source of legal and policy innovation, surpassing the interests of an exclusively Dutch audience.
